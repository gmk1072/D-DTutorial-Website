Step by Step Creating a D&D 5e Character Tutorial

When choosing armor you have to keep in mind what you have proficiency in. If you don't have proficiency in the armor you wear, you can't cast any spells. Also some armors don't use dexterity modifiers and some do, so if your dexterity modifier isn't that great, you may want to look into heavier armor to not have to rely on your dexterity modifier. If you are planning on using stealth, definitely look into medium or light armor, as most heavy and some medium armor will give disadvantage on stealth checks.

When choosing your weapon you will want to look at the damage, whether you're proficient with it, whether it is light(able to off-hand it) or finesse(dex based), and the reach of it as well as a few other things. Some of my favorite things about the weapons though is just picking one that I like, like I really like halberds so I may try to have my character use that even though something like a greatsword might have more damage.
